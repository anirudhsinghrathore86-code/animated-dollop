The Art of Rajasthani Halwai

Established in the heart of Sheoganj, Jagdish Sweets & Colddrinks is more than a shop; it’s a landmark of taste. We believe that sweets are a symbol of joy, and we craft that joy using recipes passed down through generations.

Every batch of our famous Lassi and Mithai is made with milk sourced directly from local farms, ensuring unmatched freshness.
